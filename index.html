Google Map Geocoder

What is it?

I was trying to find somewhere online that I could quickly input an address and get the geo-coordinates. I know you can do this in Google Maps, but the process just didn't seem intuitive enough for me. I wanted to make it happen in one click.

Additionally I thought it'd be handy to be able to put in coordinates and get back Google's reverse geocoding results.

How does it work?

Input an address and hit Get Coords, or enter Lat and Lng coordinates and hit Reverse Geocode.
